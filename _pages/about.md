For my master's thesis, I am designing a benchmark to evaluate **multimodal, multi-turn Deep Research Agents (DRAs)**, with a focus on systematically assessing agent reasoning, tool use, and information synthesis during long research tasks. 

**I will be attending ICML 2026** in South Korea this July to present my work, "Multi-Turn Evaluation of Deep Research Agents Under Process-Level Feedback". 
If you are attending ICML this year and want to chat, feel free to reach out via email. **I am actively looking for Agentic AI and ML Engineering roles.**

At the University of Edinburgh, I have taken relevant courses including Accelerated NLP, Advanced Topics in NLP, Machine Learning Systems, and Probabilistic Modelling and Reasoning. Through these courses, I have worked on several projects such as post-training of small-scale LLMs, Agentic RAG-based systems, optimizing an ASR system via GPU programming in Triton, and a FastAPI Safety Gateway for local LLMs.
Please refer to my [resume](https://sabharwalrishabh.github.io/assets/pdf/Rishabh_Sabharwal_Resume.pdf) for further details on my lastest projects and work experience.
